Introducing New Lexus IS 350

Photos and video

Why Lexus IS350 ?



The Lexus IS 350, a flagship model for the brand, embodies luxury, performance, and cutting-edge technology. Let's delve into the details of the 2021 model.


Design and Exterior


The 2021 Lexus IS 350 features a sleek and aerodynamic exterior design that exudes sophistication and elegance. It boasts a distinctive spindle grille, sharp LED headlights, and sculpted body lines that create a dynamic and commanding presence on the road. With available options for striking colours like Ultrasonic Blue Mica and Matador Red Mica, drivers can personalize their IS 350 to reflect their style.


Technology and Infotainment


The 2021 Lexus IS 350 comes equipped with an array of cutting-edge technology and infotainment features. The standard Lexus Multimedia System includes an 8-inch touchscreen display with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility, allowing seamless smartphone integration. Optional upgrades include a 10.3-inch display, Mark Levinson premium audio system, and built-in navigation with voice command.


Performance and Engine Specs


At the heart of the Lexus IS 350 is a potent 3.5-liter V6 engine that delivers exhilarating performance. This powertrain produces an impressive 311 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, providing robust acceleration and dynamic handling. Paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission and available rear-wheel or all-wheel drive, the IS 350 offers a thrilling driving experience with responsive power delivery and precise control.


Safety and Driver Assistance


Safety is a top priority for Lexus, and the IS 350 is equipped with a comprehensive suite of driver assistance features and advanced safety technologies. Standard safety features include Lexus Safety System+ 2.5, which includes a pre-collision system with pedestrian detection, lane departure alert with steering assist, dynamic radar cruise control, and intelligent high beams. Additionally, available options like blind spot monitoring with rear cross-traffic alert and parking assist further enhance safety and peace of mind.


Comparison to Previous Model and Competitors


Compared to its predecessor, the 2021 Lexus IS 350 boasts updated styling, enhanced performance, and advanced technology features. With its refined design, improved powertrain, and upgraded interior amenities, it sets a new standard for luxury sports sedans. When compared to competitors like the BMW 3 Series, Audi A4, and Mercedes-Benz C-Class, the Lexus IS 350 stands out with its exceptional build quality, reliability, and exceptional driving dynamics.


Price


The base price for the Lexus IS 350 2021 model typically starts around $42,000 USD, but this can vary based on factors such as optional features, trim levels, and location.


Color Options


Lexus offers a range of color options for the IS 350, including classic shades like Obsidian Black and Atomic Silver, as well as more vibrant options like Infrared and Ultrasonic Blue Mica. The availability of colors may vary depending on the dealership and market demand.


For accurate and up-to-date pricing, color options, and tax information specific to your location, I recommend contacting your local Lexus dealership or visiting their official website. They can provide you with detailed pricing quotes, available colors, and tax estimates based on your individual circumstances and preferences. In conclusion, the 2021 Lexus IS 350 offers a compelling blend of luxury, performance, and technology. Its striking design, powerful engine, and advanced features provide an exhilarating driving experience that will impress even the most discerning drivers.
